Welcome Everyone

Hello, I'm Premthip

Energetic, Adaptive, Curious and Emphatic

I was born in Thailand, the land of delicious food, sunshine, and beautiful beaches. I love spicy and Asian food and hated pizza. But I moved to Sweden and fell in love with a Swedish pizza.

During my work, I always apply and combine different perspectives to create an impact and build a satisfying solution for every stakeholder. I thrive on challenging and tackling complex problems. I enjoy the feeling of accomplishment.

Through my traveling, I discovered many mind-blowing local designs in each country. My ultimate goal is to combine a design from a different country, then create an impressive experience and show it to the world.

In my free time, I always look for an opportunity to improve and acquire new skills. From Hackathons, Seminar, Cooking to Crafting. I believe that we always learn something even from a small task.

However, having nice food and doing nothing also my favorite activities.

I wanted to be a teacher once but I end up being a product designer! But I turned that dream into one of my hobbies. I am a volunteer English and Match teacher for blind and primary students.
